Q : What do the red regions in a section view(剖視圖) in Onshape mean?

A : They represent an interference(干擾) between two parts.

Q : How do you measure the distance between two surfaces in Onshape?

A : Simply select them, and look in the bottom right-hand corner of the graphics screen.

Q : What must each part have before Onshape can report accurate(準確) Mass Properties(屬性)?

A : A material (density(密度)) assigned.

Q : What file type should be exported to 3D Print something from Onshape?

A : STL

Q : Does the Onshape mobile(移動) app allow you to edit your design via a phone or tablet, or is it just a “view-only” App? What’s another common use case?

A : It allows you to view and edit your designs, as well as comment on designs wherever you are.
