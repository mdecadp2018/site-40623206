Q : Name 2 typical real-world applications for a “Loft” feature.

A : Fan blades(風扇葉片), aircraft wings(飛機機翼), propellor(螺旋槳), other aerodynamic(空氣動力學) applications, etc.

Q : Which sketch tool is the best for tracing over curvy lines(曲線)?

A : Spline.

Q : What does it mean to “emboss(浮雕)” text onto the surface?

A : It is when the text is either raised, or sunken(凹陷), into the surface of a part.

Q : What is a common application of a “Helix(螺旋)” feature?

A : To model a spring, or a threaded surface.

Q : What does it mean to “Branch and Merge(合併)” a design in Onshape?

A : Generally speaking, “branching” and “merging” are tools used in concurrent design, where design variations are investigated(研究) at the same time. A “Branch” represents one of the several design options, and a “Merge” is when two design options are combined, where the result has the design features of both options.
